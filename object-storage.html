Object Storage

Linode's S3 equivalent — fully S3-compatible. Same endpoints, access keys and tooling (AWS CLI, s3cmd, Cyberduck) as Amazon S3.
API: /object-storage/buckets
S3-compatible. Buckets speak the S3 API, so anything targeting Amazon S3 works by swapping the endpoint to *.linodeobjects.com. Pricing is flat: $0.02/GB with a $5/mo minimum under 250 GB, and 1 TB of egress bundled in. Ideal for off-server .db backups, media, static sites and logs.
